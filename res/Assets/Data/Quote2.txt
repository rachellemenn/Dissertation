Quote
"<p>I’m sure I looked at being Jewish as being a disadvantage in my earlier years. One day things just changed, I became more and more interested in what my heritage was ... </p><p>I felt such a strong connection to the stories I was told by my bubby.</p> <br /> I knew I had to be part of a larger community. I knew I had to try to keep kosher which would have been impossible in Virginia. I knew I had to be around other Jews.</p>"
